About Me

Hello! My name is Newman Demba, and I am a passionate web developer and computer science student at BYU-Idaho. I have a background in banking and finance but transitioned into technology due to my interest in programming and software development.

I specialize in frontend and backend web development and have experience working with technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, and .NET. I am constantly learning and improving my skills to build efficient and user-friendly web applications.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy learning about new technologies, problem-solving, and spending time with my family. I am also dedicated to lifelong learning and applying my knowledge to real-world projects.

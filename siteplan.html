Site Plan: JoziTech - Online Electronics Store

Site Name

The site name is JoziTech, chosen to reflect the city of Johannesburg (Jozi) and the focus on technology (Tech). It combines a local and modern feel, which is important for branding and visibility in the local market.

Optional domain availability: jozi-tech.com (for reference)

Site Purpose

The purpose of the JoziTech website is to provide a seamless shopping experience for customers who want to purchase electronics and gadgets in Johannesburg. The website will offer product catalogs, secure checkout, and customer support, with a focus on a clean, user-friendly design.

Scenarios

  • How can I find the best deals on laptops and smartphones in Johannesburg?
  • How do I track my order after making a purchase?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #0066cc (used for headings, navigation, and buttons)

Secondary Color: #e5e5e5 (used for background and sections)

Typography

Primary Font: Arial, sans-serif (used for body text and general content)

Secondary Font: Georgia, serif (used for headings and titles)
